LatticeOS

Revive. Repurpose. Reconnect.

LatticeOS is a lightweight operating system designed to breathe new life into legacy devices. Built with empathy, reuse, and resilience in mind — LatticeOS empowers users to reclaim their tech and reconnect with purpose.

We believe the tools we use should serve people, not the other way around. Our mission is to make computing sustainable, personal, and free.
